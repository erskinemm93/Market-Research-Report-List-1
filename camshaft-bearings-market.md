<p><strong>The Camshaft Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) Bearings</li><li>Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camshaft bearings market is segmented into two main types: Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) bearings and Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) bearings. SOHC bearings are used in engines featuring a single camshaft that operates both intake and exhaust valves, providing a simpler design and reduced weight. In contrast, DOHC bearings accommodate two camshafts for separate control of intake and exhaust valves, enhancing performance and efficiency. This differentiation caters to varied automotive applications, influencing market demand based on engine design preferences.</p></p>
