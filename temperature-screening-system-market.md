<p><ul><li>Mass Screening</li><li>Precise Screening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature screening system market is categorized into two main types: mass screening and precise screening. Mass screening involves the rapid assessment of large groups, utilizing infrared cameras or thermal scanners to quickly identify individuals with elevated temperatures, often in public places like airports or events. In contrast, precise screening focuses on detailed assessments of individuals using handheld devices or specialized equipment for more accurate temperature readings, typically employed in healthcare settings or at points of entry for thorough evaluations.</p></p>
