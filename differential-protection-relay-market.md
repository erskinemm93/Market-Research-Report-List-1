<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Differential Protection Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Differential Relay</li><li>Biased or Percentage Differential Relay</li><li>Voltage Balance Differential Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The differential protection relay market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Current Differential Relay** uses current transformers to compare input and output currents, detecting imbalances that indicate faults. </p><p>2. **Biased or Percentage Differential Relay** incorporates a biasing feature to prevent false tripping during normal load variations, allowing a percentage difference in current. </p><p>3. **Voltage Balance Differential Relay** monitors voltage levels across different circuits to identify discrepancies, ensuring effective fault detection. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing electrical safety and reliability.</p></p>
