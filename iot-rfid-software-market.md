<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID software market is primarily categorized into passive and active RFID types. Passive RFID utilizes tags that do not have their own power source; they rely on energy from the reader to transmit data, making them cost-effective and ideal for inventory tracking. In contrast, active RFID features battery-powered tags that can broadcast signals independently, enabling real-time location tracking and monitoring over longer distances. Both types enhance supply chain efficiency and asset management across various industries.</p></p>
